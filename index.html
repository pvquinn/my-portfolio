About Me

Hi, my name is Pillar Quinn, and I am a soon-to-be graduate of Washington State University and Spokane Community College where I am earning my associate's degree in software development and my bachelor's degree in biology. I have a strong passion for software development and am eager to start my career in this field. I am particularly interested in working in Germany, and am willing to do what it takes to secure a work permit in order to make this dream a reality.

During my time at Spokane Community College, I have gained a wide range of technical skills in full stack web development, Android development, RESTful APIs, and SQL and NoSQL databases. I have also earned several certificates in back end development, mobile development, React, Javascript, front end development, and full stack development, both from college and online from Scrimba. Prior to studying software development, I studied pharmacy for three years and have experience working in pharmaceutical research.

Outside of my studies, I enjoy riding bikes and singing. I am a focused, thorough, and determined employee who has a proven track record of success, including being named employee of the year at a previous job. I am confident in my problem-solving abilities and am always eager to take on new challenges.
